## As a user

- Create/edit/view/delete an account
- Sign in
- Sign out
- Upload a profile picture
- View all travel posts
- Create/edit/view/delete a travel post with details
- Comment on other travel posts
- Join other trips/posts
- Like other posts
- View your trips
